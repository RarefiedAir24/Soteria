# Plaid Application - Reason for Access

When Plaid asks for a reason for access during account creation, use one of these options:

## Recommended Reason (Primary)

**"Personal finance management and savings goal tracking - helping users transfer money to savings accounts when they avoid impulse purchases"**

## Alternative Reasons

1. **"Behavioral finance app to help users save money by tracking spending patterns and enabling automatic transfers to savings goals"**

2. **"Financial wellness app that helps users build savings habits by connecting bank accounts to transfer money to savings goals"**

3. **"Personal finance tool for savings goal management and automated transfers"**

## What to Select

- **Use case**: Personal Finance Management / Savings Goals
- **Product type**: Consumer-facing app
- **Integration type**: Mobile app (iOS)

## Key Points to Emphasize

- ✅ Helping users save money
- ✅ Personal finance management
- ✅ Savings goal tracking
- ✅ Transfer functionality (moving money to savings)
- ✅ Consumer-facing mobile app

## What NOT to Say

- ❌ "Investment advice" (unless you're licensed)
- ❌ "Lending" (unless you're a lender)
- ❌ "Cryptocurrency trading"
- ❌ Anything that sounds like you're a financial institution

## After Approval

Once approved, you'll get access to:
- Sandbox environment (immediate)
- Development environment (may require additional approval)
- Production environment (requires full review)

For now, **Sandbox is sufficient** for testing your integration!
